Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Subaru auto spare parts in Kenya

Where can I find genuine Subaru spare parts in Kenya?

We offer a wide range of genuine Subaru spare parts sourced directly from trusted suppliers. Our store in Kenya stocks everything from engines to small components, ensuring you get quality parts for your Subaru vehicle.

How do I know if a part is compatible with my Subaru model?

Our experienced staff can assist you in verifying the compatibility of parts with your specific Subaru model. You can also use our online search tool by entering your car's make and model to find the correct parts.

Do you offer delivery services across Kenya?

Yes, we offer reliable delivery services to all parts of Kenya. Whether you are in Nairobi, Mombasa, or any other region, we ensure your Subaru spare parts reach you promptly and safely.

Can I return a spare part if it does not fit my vehicle?

We have a return policy in place for spare parts that do not fit your vehicle. Please ensure you return the part in its original condition and packaging within the specified return period. Our team will guide you through the return process.

What should I do if I can't find the Subaru part I'm looking for?

If you can't find the Subaru spare part you need, please contact us. We can source it for you or provide alternatives that are compatible with your vehicle. Our team is committed to ensuring you get the best solution for your Subaru.
